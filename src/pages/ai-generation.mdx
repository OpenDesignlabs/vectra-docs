# AI Generation

Vectra has a built-in AI that can generate page layouts from a text description. Instead of starting from a blank canvas, describe what you want and the AI builds a working starting point in seconds.

## How to use it

**1. Open the AI panel.**
Click the sparkle icon `✦` in the left toolbar, or press `Ctrl + K`.

**2. Type your prompt.**
Describe the page or section you want. Be specific — the more detail you give, the closer the result will be to what you're imagining.

**3. Hit Generate.**
Vectra's AI builds the layout and places it on your canvas. This usually takes a few seconds.

**4. Edit the result.**
The generated layout is fully editable. Click any element to change its text, color, or position. Use it as a starting point, not a final design.

## Writing good prompts

The AI responds well to prompts that mention:

- **The type of page** — landing page, pricing page, about page, contact form
- **The sections you want** — hero, features grid, testimonials, FAQ, footer
- **The style** — minimal, bold, dark background, lots of whitespace, colorful
- **Your product or service** — the more context, the better the copy

**Examples of good prompts:**

> "A dark landing page for a productivity app. Hero section with a headline and CTA button, then a three-column features section, then a pricing table with two plans."

> "A simple about page for a design studio. Team photo placeholder at the top, a two-paragraph bio, and a contact button."

> "A minimal portfolio homepage. Just a large name, job title, short bio, and links to Twitter and LinkedIn."

<Callout type="tip">
  If the first result isn't what you wanted, refine the prompt and generate again. You can also keep the parts you like and re-generate only a specific section.
</Callout>

## What AI generation is not

The AI generates *layout and structure* — containers, headings, buttons, sections in the right arrangement. It is not a design system. You'll want to:

- Replace placeholder text with your real copy
- Swap in your actual images
- Adjust colors to match your brand using the Theme panel
- Fine-tune spacing and typography to your preference

Think of it as a very fast first draft, not a finished product.
