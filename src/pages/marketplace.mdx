# The Marketplace

The Marketplace is Vectra's integrated library of high-quality, production-ready components. Instead of building every section from scratch, you can browse a growing catalog of headers, heroes, footers, and complex features.

## Why use Marketplace components?

Every component in the Marketplace is built using the same primitives you use in the Studio:
- **Pure React:** No bloated wrappers or proprietary logic.
- **Tailwind CSS:** Fully styled with Tailwind utility classes.
- **Modifiable:** Once inserted, the component is fully "unlocked" and editable on your canvas.

## The Workflow

### 1. Browse and Preview
Use the sidebar in the Marketplace to filter by category. When you find a component you like, click the **Live Preview** toggle to see it in a responsive iframe.

<Callout type="info">
  **Real Breakpoint Testing:** The Marketplace preview lets you freely resize the component to verify it looks great on mobile, tablet, and desktop before you commit to it.
</Callout>

### 2. Check Official Status
Look for the **Official** badge `✦`. These components are maintained by the Vectra team and are guaranteed to follow our latest design system standards.

### 3. One-Click Insert
Click **Insert** to download the component binary and its source code into your project. It will appear at the bottom of your currently active canvas section.

### 4. Direct Editing
Once a Marketplace section is on your canvas, select it to view its properties in the right panel.
- **Props:** Change text, images, and links via the interactive schema.
- **Styles:** Swap Tailwind classes directly on the element's nodes.
- **Structure:** Add or remove sub-elements within the imported component.

---

## Publishing to the Marketplace

Are you a developer who has built something amazing? You can share it!

1. Select a component or section on your canvas.
2. Click the **Publish** icon in the context menu.
3. Add a label, description, and tags.
4. Your component will be available for others (or just your team) to use.
